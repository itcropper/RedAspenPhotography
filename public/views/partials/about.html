About My Photography

I remember begging my parents for a film camera on my 6th birthday and once I got that present, it didn’t leave my side. My grandmother told me a few years back, “we always knew you were the creative one and your career would take you in that direction.” I, unfortunately lost sight of that when entering into college and was determined to climb the corporate ladder in a very different career path. Some may say I jumped off that ladder, while others tell me they knew I was destined to take a side road into a new wide open field. And here I am!

My first digital camera was gifted to me by my husband! I find an immense amount of joy from being a part of your life for a moment in time. My photography is very relaxed and allows for capturing the subtle yet important pieces of life. I love the shared opportunity for me to be welcomed into your life and provide a memory for you. I once had a client that said to me, “How wonderful it must be when you are a photography. You are the one who gets to go home and view those special moments and see them again for the first time.” And that is exactly why I love being a photographer.
